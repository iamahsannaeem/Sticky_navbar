Why we Need Stick Navbar

A sticky navbar is essential for improving website usability and navigation by providing a consistent menu that remains visible as users scroll. It enhances user experience by offering quick access to key sections, promoting easy navigation, and reducing the need to scroll back to the top. This helps users find content efficiently and promotes engagement. It also offers a sense of orientation and continuity, making the website more user-friendly and accessible.
